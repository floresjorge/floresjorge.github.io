Transforming Raw Sales Data

Using an anonymized online sales dataset from Kaggle, I conducted a structured analysis to extract valuable business insights. The process began with Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), identifying missing values, validating quantities, and assessing data integrity. Next, I performed data cleaning with SQL queries, filtering out invalid entries and creating a new revenue column. This refined dataset became the foundation for a dynamic dashboard in Looker Studio. To facilitate deeper insights, I aggregated key sales metrics, including sales by country, category, item, and average order value. Finally, I structured an annual sales analysis, revealing year-over-year trends across multiple dimensions.
